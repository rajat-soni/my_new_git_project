Methods to work with attributes are: elem.hasAttribute(name) – to check for existence. elem.getAttribute(name) – to get the value. elem.setAttribute(name, value) – to set the value. elem.removeAttribute(name) – to remove the attribute. elem.attributes is a collection of all attributes.
